Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: April 2025

We want you to be 100% satisfied with your purchase. If there is any issue with your order, we are here to help. Please contact us within 7 days of receiving the product.

1. Return Eligibility

You can return a product if:

  • The product arrived damaged or defective
  • The wrong product was delivered
  • The product is not working on arrival (Dead on Arrival)
  • The product is significantly different from what was described

Returns are NOT accepted for:

  • Products damaged due to incorrect installation by the customer
  • Products damaged due to power fluctuations or incorrect voltage
  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Custom-made orders (made to order products)
  • Products reported after 7 days of delivery

2. How to Raise a Return Request

1
WhatsApp us at +91 93928 78946 or email hello@signsandarts.in within 7 days of delivery
2
Share your Order ID, photos or video of the damaged/defective product
3
Our team will review and respond within 24-48 hours
4
If approved, we will arrange pickup or ask you to ship the product back
5
Replacement or refund will be processed within 5-7 working days

3. Refund Policy

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 working days
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method (UPI, bank account, or card)
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • For prepaid orders, the refund amount will be the product price minus shipping charges

4. Replacement Policy

  • We offer free replacement for defective or damaged products
  • Replacement will be dispatched within 24-48 hours of return approval
  • Replacement is subject to product availability

5. Warranty

  • All Signs and Arts LED sign boards come with a 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Warranty does not cover physical damage, water damage (beyond rated weatherproofing), or damage due to incorrect voltage
  • To claim warranty, contact us with your order details and proof of purchase

6. Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement
  • Once dispatched, orders cannot be cancelled
  • To cancel, WhatsApp us immediately at +91 93928 78946
  • Cancelled order refunds are processed within 3-5 working days

7. Contact Us for Returns

  • WhatsApp: +91 93928 78946 (10am-8pm, Mon-Sat)
  • Email: hello@signsandarts.in
  • Website: signsandarts.in
